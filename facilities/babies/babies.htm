Babies and Toddlers

We have high quality equipment to ensure your little ones comfort and safety. Proper wooden cots, highchairs, stairgates, bedgates, booster seat cushions, bottle sterilisers, small cutlery, plates, baby baths are all available at no extra cost. A single bed may be substituted for our car or sheep bed.

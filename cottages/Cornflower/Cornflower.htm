Cornflower cottage

Cornflower is a cosy haven. Renovated throughout and furnished to a high standard. The high A frame ceiling and cottage style windows add to the ambiance.

Reached by an enclosed private sunny facing garden. The living area has comfortable sofas, flat screen TV and DVD player. Books and toys may be borrowed from the beach hut.

The kitchen has a comprehensive range of cook and table ware with fridge and ice box, hob, oven and microwave.

The fresh, soothing double bedroom overlooks the lake and meadows. There is generous hanging and storage space. iPod docking station and hair dryer.

A twin bedroom provides two full sized single beds and ample storage.

The bathroom has a bath with overhead power shower, sink and w.c.

Cornflower has oil central heating. Towels and bed linen are provided. All electricity, heating and WiFi are included in the tariff.

Smoking is not permitted inside the property.
